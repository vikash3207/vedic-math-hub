## ğŸ•‰ï¸ Origin of Vedic Maths

Vedic Maths is based on **ancient Indian mathematical principles**, reconstructed and systematized by **Swami Bharati Krishna Tirthaji (1884-1960)** between 1911 and 1918.

He compiled **16 Sutras (main formulas)** and **13 Sub-Sutras (corollaries)**, which he claimed were derived from the **Vedas**. These principles apply to various branches of mathematics including arithmetic, algebra, geometry, calculus, and more.

**The 16 Sutras** provide mental calculation techniques that simplify complex mathematical operations.
